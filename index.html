About Conditor

One studio, every stage of the build.

Conditor is a full-service architectural and engineering consultancy. We take a project from first concept through council-ready drawings, then stay involved through sourcing, construction and finishing — so nothing gets lost in the handoff between disciplines.

Every drawing set is built to be practical as well as beautiful: modelled for drainage and structure, checked against local authority requirements, and detailed enough to walk straight onto site.

The plan is only half the drawing.

What the schedule can't show is how the light moves through it — how a cantilevered edge throws shade over the terrace by early afternoon, or how a single planted corner softens a very deliberate grid of concrete and glass.

Conditor pairs each floor plan with an elevation built to hold up outside the drawing, not just inside it. The Alcove's massing is designed to read this way from the street: layered, quiet, and unmistakably considered.

06 — Full-Service Delivery

Architectural Design & Engineering Consultancy

Whether you're planning a new home, commercial development or industrial facility, we turn your vision into a professionally designed project that is ready for approval and construction.

Our team prepares coordinated architectural and engineering drawings that comply with local authority requirements, helping you navigate the approval process with confidence while ensuring every design is practical, efficient and buildable.

Our comprehensive design service includes architectural plans, 3D visualisations, structural engineering drawings and calculations, drainage and water reticulation layouts, landscape design, site planning, construction details, material specifications and council submission documentation. From the first concept through statutory approvals and construction support, we provide everything you need under one roof — giving you a seamless experience from idea to completed project.
